🧠 Tokenization Simulator

At its core, tokenization is the process of converting ownership of real-world assets into digital tokens on a blockchain. These tokens represent fractional ownership, making it possible for anyone—not just institutions—to access and invest in high-value assets. This shift unlocks liquidity, enables 24/7 trading, and removes geographic barriers that have historically limited participation.
